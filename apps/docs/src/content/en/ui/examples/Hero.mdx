Examples of creating hero content sections.


{/* `<Hero>` combined with `<HeroContent>` allows content to be placed at the top, center, or bottom. */}
{/* <p>Control top, center, and bottom alignment of body blocks from the parent block. (by changing margin)</p>
<p>Allow selecting the initially expanded block. Also support internal grid patterns.</p> */}
