### Specifying Start and End, highlighting shapes

Adding `isStart` to the first item and `isEnd` to the last item removes the overflowing line at the top and bottom respectively.
Additionally, `isHighlighted` enables highlighted display, and `shapeColor` specifies the shape color.


<Preview>
  <PreviewTitle>Usage example</PreviewTitle>
  <PreviewArea p='20'>
    <Timeline.Root>
      <Timeline.Item isStart>
        <Timeline.Time>Month dd, 20yy</Timeline.Time>
        <Timeline.Text>
          <Text>Lism x.y.z released.</Text>
          <Dummy lang="ja" />
        </Timeline.Text>
      </Timeline.Item>
      <Timeline.Item>
        <Timeline.Time>Month dd, 20yy</Timeline.Time>
        <Timeline.Text>
          <Text>Lism x.y.z released.</Text>
          <Dummy lang="ja" />
        </Timeline.Text>
      </Timeline.Item>
      <Timeline.Item isEnd isHighlighted shapeColor="red">
        <Timeline.Time>Month dd, 20yy</Timeline.Time>
        <Timeline.Text>
          <Text>Lism x.y.z released.</Text>
          <Dummy lang="ja" />
        </Timeline.Text>
      </Timeline.Item>
    </Timeline.Root>
  </PreviewArea>
  <PreviewCode>
```jsx "isStart" "isEnd" "isHighlighted" "shapeColor"
<Timeline.Root>
  <Timeline.Item isStart>
    <Timeline.Time>Month dd, 20yy</Timeline.Time>

      <Dummy lang="ja" />
    </Timeline.Text>
  </Timeline.Item>
  <Timeline.Item>
    <Timeline.Time>Month dd, 20yy</Timeline.Time>
```
